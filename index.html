About NurMed

NurMed is a visionary global healthcare system powered by blockchain. Our goal is to bring trusted, secure, and accessible healthcare services to every corner of the world — starting with Pakistan. We believe healthcare should be decentralized, transparent, and always within reach.

Vision & Mission

Vision: To create a decentralized, patient-first global healthcare ecosystem.

Mission: Empowering communities through digital medical infrastructure, trust, and innovation.

How NurMed Works

  • Blockchain-based patient records and transparency
  • Mobile access to real-time medical assistance
  • AI-powered diagnostics and recommendations
  • Telehealth integration with certified doctors

Why Choose NurMed?

Unlike traditional hospital chains, NurMed focuses on technological accessibility, ethical care, and transparent systems that respect patient ownership over data and health outcomes.

Future Roadmap

  1. Launch pilot model in Khyber Pakhtunkhwa
  2. Expand to national level clinics and mobile units
  3. Build cross-border healthcare data exchange platform
  4. Develop NurMed Hospitals and Smart Centers globally
